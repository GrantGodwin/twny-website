# The Signature Component — *The Operating Layer*

**Status:** First definition of the element the [Art Direction](../../governance/art-direction.md#the-signature-component)
requires to be *discovered before pages are built.* Proposed and now implemented as
`src/components/home/OperatingLayer.astro`; open to revision.

## The job
Express, in one calm composition, **the connected operating layer behind a business** — the
idea that the mailbox, the website, the domain, the backup, and the everyday work are
separate things **held together and looked after as one.** It is the visual sentence for the
brand philosophy — *simplicity as the outcome*: many moving parts, made to feel like one
quiet whole, under one accountable operator.

## The idea, in one line
> Several real systems, **threaded onto a single Mineral line.** The thread is the operator.

## Form
- **A single continuous Mineral thread** runs through the section — the "current" that
  connects everything (Mineral is the technology current per the colour system). It is the
  one distinctive, ownable mark.
- **A few honest operational entries** hang off the thread — Microsoft 365, website, domain,
  backup, automation — each a short Steel label + one plain line of evidence. Real, cropped,
  calm; never fake metrics.
- **A large editorial statement alongside** makes the point in words ("Separate systems. One
  operation.").
- Set on a **lifted-Ink panel** with generous space, asymmetric, type-led.

## What it is *not* (hard constraints from governance)
- Not a **fake dashboard** (no chrome, no fabricated UI, no metrics theatre).
- Not a **card grid** or a **row of chips**.
- Not an **Atlas-style diagram or flowchart** (no process arrows, no 01→02→03 steps — that is
  the operating *loop*, which is Atlas surface and belongs elsewhere, demoted).
- Not dependent on motion — it must hold as a **still image**.

## Why this and not the loop
The reference's most distinctive moment is the 01–06 operating-loop stepper. That is the
*internal method* (Discover→Operate→Review) — Atlas surface, and explicitly not the public
identity. The signature component instead binds the **services** (the offer) into one
operation. It says *"one connected thing,"* which is the differentiator, without leaking the
engine room.

## Where it lives
The **keystone moment of the homepage** (purpose 3: *show how TWNY simplifies it*), and the
visual logic it establishes — one Mineral thread connecting honest fragments — can recur on
service pages as the shared "hand". It replaces the card-grid "offer" and the loop-stepper as
the page's signature.
